​This Privacy Policy describes how AppDrawer Ultimate ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile/web application.
​1. Information We Collect
​We collect information to provide a better experience for our users:
​Authentication Data: If you choose to sign in via Google, we receive your name, profile picture, and email address via Firebase Authentication.
​Usage Statistics: We store local data regarding your app usage, such as the number of calculations performed and game wins (e.g., Tic Tac Toe).

​To personalize your profile and display your name/avatar within the app.

​The App integrates with several third-party providers that may collect data used to identify you:

​4. Data Storage (Local & Cloud)
​LocalStorage: Most of your data (Calculation history, game stats, and theme settings) is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.
​Cloud Storage: Only your basic profile information is handled through Firebase when you are logged in.
​5. Children’s Privacy
​AppDrawer Ultimate does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent and aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

​We value your trust in providing us with your information. While we use industry-standard tools like Google Firebase to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
